General Elden Ring Information

Elden Ring is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, known for his work on the Souls series, and is set in a fantasy world created by Miyazaki and author George R. R. Martin (Author of the Game of Thrones series). Elden Ring won four game awards including Game of the Year in 2022.
