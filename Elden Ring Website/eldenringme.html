Just thought I would share some of my Elden Ring stats! My first playthrough I played as an Astrologer. It was my first From FromSoftware game so I didn't know what to expect. I chose the astrologer so that I could play from a distance and negate most of the hand-to-hand combat. Playing as a mage was great fun and I ended up playing up to new game plus 3 with that character. My next playthrough I decided to play as other mage the prophet! The prophet was fun andn the playstyle was very similar to the astrologer just with some new type of magic and some new weapons. I was actually able to get closer in combat and kill some bosses without magic in this playthrough. The character I'm currently playing and using on stream is a wretch. I created a character from scratch and I've only been upgrading Strength and Vigor(health). I use a giant stone hammer and I'm having the time of my life with it. The strength build definately feels more satisfying when I kill bosses because I actually have to learn their moveset. Love this game and can't wait for the DLC!
