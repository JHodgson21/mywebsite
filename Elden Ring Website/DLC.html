DLC "Shadow of the Erd Tree" Coming June 21st

Elden Ring is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, known for his work on the Souls series, and is set in a fantasy world created by Miyazaki and author George R. R. Martin(Author of the Game of Thrones series).

News!

Two years after the release of Elden Ring, the DLC is finally coming! On June 21st the DLC will be out! Not much is known about what level each character will start out as but it will be great. I believe that no matter what level you are going into the dlc the enemies will be scaled to your character. The dlc is going to be 12GB compared to the base game which is 48GB. Miyazaki said that the dlc map will be larger than Limgrave. So if we put that into perspective it would be from the most southern tip of Limgrave all the way up to Godricks castle. Pretty decent size if you ask me. Here is a link to the DLC trailer!

Messmer The Impaler

The dlc is centered around this enemy Messmer The Impaler. Little is know about him but we have confirmed that this is not Melenia's brother. Which kind of makes me sad. However Miyazaki said that this boss battle probably won't be to the same scale as the Melania fight but that there will be other bosses not part of the main story that will be hard. Knowing FromSoftware dlcs some of the hardest bosses of the series comes from the dlc, so it should be a lot of fun!
