General Elden Ring Information

Elden Ring is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, known for his work on the Souls series, and is set in a fantasy world created by Miyazaki and author George R. R. Martin(Author of the Game of Thrones series).

George R. R. Martin

George R. R. Martin, an American author who most noteably wrote the Game of Thrones series, took sometime away from the series to help Hidetaka Miyazaki write the story for Elden Ring. The story takes a lonley Tarnished (the player) and puts them on a quest for the Elden Ring. The realm of The Lands Between are in utter chaos. Power hungry many gods, demi-gods, and demons are in search for the power to control the land. It's all up to the Tarnished to restore order to the land.

Awards

Elden Ring won many gaming awards including the prestigeous 2022 Game of the Year award. Just to name a few: Best Game Direction, Best Art Direction, Best RPG of the Year. When I first played Elden Ring I was stunned by the sure quality and beauty of the game. There were too many times that I was playing and I just had to stop and take in the beautiful atmosphere that I was in.
