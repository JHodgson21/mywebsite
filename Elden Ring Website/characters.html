Character Gallery

The Hero is the perfect Hack'n slash character. The Hero's main two attributes are Health and Strength.

The bandit is a light fast character. The bandit foucues on dexterity and arcane. Great for elemental build types. Just dont get hit while playing the bandit!

The main spell-caster of the group. The astrologer is a glass cannon, does incredible damage but has no health. Keep your distance!

The Warrior is a pure dexterity type. Great class if you want to dual wield swords, axes, or katanas!

The prisoner is an interesting character. The only other character to start off with high intelligence. He can cast spells like the astrologer but also has a straight sword for close combat.

The Confessor focues on Faith/Strength builds. This would be a perfect starting character for a Holy Paladin!

The Wretch is for the experienced players who would like to create their character from scratch instead of having preset attributes. Very fun, but the start of the game is much harder this way.

The Vagabond is the tank of the group. Great starting health and great starting damage. If you want to swing around the biggest swords and hammers in the game chose the Vagabond!

The prophet is another mage type. The prophet specializes in Faith, faith magic is magic from your body not a staff. This includes fireballs, lighting spears, and so many buffs that you can apply to yourself.

The Samurai gets upclose and personal. No magic attributes but the Samurai is strong, has great health, and has tons of endurance. The Samurai also carries a long bow for long ranged attacks.
