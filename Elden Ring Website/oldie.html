General Elden Ring Information

Elden Ring is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, known for his work on the Souls series, and is set in a fantasy world created by Miyazaki and author George R. R. Martin(Author of the Game of Thrones series).

George R. R. Martin

George R. R. Martin, an American author who most notably wrote the Game of Thrones series, took some time away from the series to help Hidetaka Miyazaki write the story for Elden Ring. The story takes a lonely Tarnished (the player) and puts them on a quest for the Elden Ring. The realm of The Lands Between is in utter chaos. Power-hungry gods, demi-gods, and demons are in search of the power to control the land. It's all up to the Tarnished to restore order to the land.

Awards

Elden Ring won many gaming awards including the prestigious 2022 Game of the Year award. Just to name a few: Best Game Direction, Best Art Direction, Best RPG of the Year. When I first played Elden Ring I was stunned by the sheer quality and beauty of the game. There were too many times that I was playing and I just had to stop and take in the beautiful atmosphere that I was in.

CHARACTERS

The Hero is the perfect Hack'n slash character. The Hero's main two attributes are Health and Strength.

The bandit is a light fast character. The bandit focuses on dexterity and arcane. Great for elemental build types. Just don't get hit while playing the bandit!

The main spell-caster of the group. The astrologer is a glass cannon, does incredible damage but has no health. Keep your distance!

The Warrior is a pure dexterity type. Great class if you want to dual wield swords, axes, or katanas!

The prisoner is an interesting character. The only other character to start off with high intelligence. He can cast spells like the astrologer but also has a straight sword for close combat.

The Confessor focuses on Faith/Strength builds. This would be a perfect starting character for a Holy Paladin!

The Wretch is for the experienced players who would like to create their character from scratch instead of having preset attributes. Very fun, but the start of the game is much harder this way.

The Vagabond is the tank of the group. Great starting health and great starting damage. If you want to swing around the biggest swords and hammers in the game choose the Vagabond!

The prophet is another mage type. The prophet specializes in Faith, faith magic is magic from your body not a staff. This includes fireballs, lighting spears, and so many buffs that you can apply to yourself.

The Samurai gets up close and personal. No magic attributes but the Samurai is strong, has great health, and has tons of endurance. The Samurai also carries a long bow for long-ranged attacks.
